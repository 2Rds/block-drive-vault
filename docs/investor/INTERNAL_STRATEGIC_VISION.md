## The Macro Thesis

### The Great Migration

The global financial system ($800+ trillion in assets) is transitioning from legacy infrastructure to blockchain rails. This isn't speculation—it's happening:

- **BlackRock:** iShares Bitcoin ETF, BUIDL tokenized fund, Larry Fink's public statements
- **Fidelity:** Crypto custody, tokenized fund initiatives
- **JPMorgan:** Onyx blockchain platform, tokenized repo transactions
- **Franklin Templeton:** On-chain money market fund

### What They're All Missing

Every major financial institution building on-chain infrastructure needs:

1. **Compliant document management** — Prospectuses, agreements, regulatory filings
2. **Integrated settlement** — Stablecoin payments tied to document workflows
3. **Audit infrastructure** — Immutable trails for regulators
4. **Yield optimization** — Returns on document-adjacent deposits

**No one is building this layer.** The document infrastructure for institutional tokenization doesn't exist.
